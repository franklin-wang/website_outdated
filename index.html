About Me

Hi, I'm Franklin! I'm a senior majoring in Astronomy at the University of Illinois. After obtaining my bachelors degree, I intend on attending graduate school to pursue a Ph.D. in Astronomy. I'm most interested in observational astronomy, particularly in fields related to galactic evolution, active galactic nuclei, and compact objects. I'm currently working with Professor Xin Liu and Ph.D. student Colin Burke on testing a novel method to weigh intermediate-mass black holes. More information can be found on the research tab of my website.
