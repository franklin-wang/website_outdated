About Me

Hi, I'm Franklin! I'm a senior majoring in Astronomy at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I'm currently working with Professor Xin Liu and Ph.D. student Colin Burke on developing and testing a novel method to weigh intermediate-mass black holes with light curves from the Zwicky Transient Facility (ZTF). I have previously worked with Professor Tjonnie G. F. Li at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I studied gravitational waves. I will begin to pursue a Ph.D. in Astronomy at Texas A&M University in the fall of 2022!
